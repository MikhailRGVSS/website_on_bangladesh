Bangladesh

The total population is 168,697,184. The country consists of 99% Bengali people and the remaining 1% is other ethnic Indigenous groups. Bangla is spoken by almost everyone. More than 90% of the country is Muslim, with the remaining percentages belonging to Hinduism and other religions. Around 67% of the population is between the ages of 15 and 64, and 25% belonging to those between 0 and 14.
