Education in Bangladesh

The school system is very similar to Canada, as it has 3 main levels of education, primary, secondary, and tertiary education. However, they have exams at the end of every year, and they have a similar branch but they incorporate religious studies into the curriculum. After tertiary education, one can go into college and university. They have improved their education a lot in the 21st century. Less than half the population could read or write around the 2000's, but by the late 2010's, more than two-thirds of the population were literate. There are over 100 universities and colleges in Bangladesh.

University of Dhaka

The University of Dhaka was founded in 1921, when they were under British colonial rule making it the oldest university in Bangladesh. Their graduates have been very important for shaping Bangladesh's future. Initially starting off small, now it is a major university with over 37 thousand students.

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)

The Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology is the best choice for those pursuing engineering and architectural studies for students. It was established in 1971, but it went through many other names before this.
